Welcome to SP Open House's EDUCATION AND CAREER GUIDANCE (ECG) COUNSELLING Appointment page.

Book a slot to speak with our ECG Counsellors for guidance on your career interests. You can choose to have a 1-on-1 session or a small group session (up to 3 persons in the group).

To have a more meaningful conversation around your career interests, we will need you to enter your RIASEC Code in the booking form. If you do not know or have forgotten your RIASEC code, complete the O*NET Interest Profiler! at https://www.mynextmove.org/explore/ip first before booking your appointment.

How to book an ECG appointment

Choose the option that suits you best!

  • 1-1 Guidance @ 30 min per session - available in the morning only (e.g. student may attend this session with a parent/ parents)

  • Small Group Guidance @ 50 min per session - available in the afternoon only (e.g. student may book this session with friends; up to 3 students per group)
